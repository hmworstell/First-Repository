Favorite CD Releases

COIN (2015)
The debut album introduces COIN’s indie pop sound with upbeat rhythms. Highlights include “Run” and “Talk Too Much.”
How Will You Know If You Never Try (2017)
A more mature sound than their debut, this album features infectious melodies and heartfelt lyrics. Standout tracks: “I Want It All” and “Malibu 1992.”
Dreamland (2020)
This album blends dreamy synths with COIN’s signature indie pop style. “Cemetery” and “Crash My Car” are memorable and showcase the band’s growth.
